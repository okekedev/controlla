Privacy Policy

Controlla - Wireless Mac Control

Last Updated: January 2025

Overview

Controlla is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We collect NO personal information.

Controlla operates entirely on your local WiFi network and does not:

  • Collect any personal data
  • Track your usage
  • Access your files or documents
  • Store any information on our servers (we don't have servers)
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Share data with third parties

How Controlla Works

Controlla uses local network communication between your iPhone/iPad and Mac:

  • Bonjour/mDNS: Used to discover your Mac on the local network
  • WiFi Direct: All communication stays on your local network
  • No Internet Required: The app works completely offline

Permissions

Controlla requests the following permissions:

  • Local Network Access (iOS): Required to discover and connect to your Mac
  • Accessibility (macOS): Required to simulate keyboard and mouse input on your Mac

These permissions are used exclusively for the app's core functionality and never for data collection.

Subscription Information

If you subscribe to Controlla Pro:

  • Your subscription is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store
  • We receive only anonymized transaction data from Apple
  • We never see your payment information
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID settings

Data Storage

Controlla stores minimal data locally on your device:

  • App preferences (Controller or Receiver mode)
  • Subscription status (from Apple)

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

Third-Party Services

Controlla does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

Children's Privacy

Controlla does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
